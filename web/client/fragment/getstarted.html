I am a driver

... and I am willing to share my car with others and drive them to an agreed destination in exchange for a fee I decide.

1. Post a ride
Download the Obyte wallet and use it to log in to ORider, enter the pick-up and drop-off locations and departure time, price it and post it.
2. Start boarding
Drive to the pick up location, select your ride and start boarding. Wait for your passengers to arrive, let them check-in by scanning your boarding QR code.
3. Drive and get paid
Once your passengers checked-in and made a payment, drive to the drop-off location to complete the ride. Wait for the funds to be released from smart contracts into your account.


I am a passenger

... and I am willing to pay a fee and share someone's car to get to a given destination.

1. Pick a ride
Download the Obyte wallet and use it to log in, reserve a ride.
2. Check-in
Go to the pick up location, get in the car and check-in by scanning the boarding QR code with your Obyte wallet, make a payment to the smart contract that is automatically offered to you.
3. Enjoy the ride
Sit back and enjoy the ride, when you arrive at the drop-off location, leave the car. Your payment will be automatically transferred to the driver.
